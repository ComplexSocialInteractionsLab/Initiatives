Our Mission

Secure Intelligence for the Public Good

The CSI Lab develops cutting-edge, privacy-preserving AI architectures specifically designed for environments where data egress is not an option. We train the next generation of researchers and law enforcement professionals to leverage these powerful tools locally, securely, and ethically.

Air-Gapped Ecosystems

Building infrastructures that run powerful Large Language Models (LLMs) entirely on local, institutional hardware, ensuring zero data leakage to public clouds.

Workforce Development

Upskilling academics, analysts, and officers through hands-on training platforms, making complex AI systems accessible without requiring a computer science degree.

Applied Research

Deploying local AI agents to assist in processing vast amounts of unstructured data, from bodycam footage to complex financial records, accelerating critical investigations.
